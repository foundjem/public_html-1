Genetics of developmental disorders

Identify genetic causes of developmental disorders by statistical genetics of rare variants and integration of biological mechanisms. We collaborate with Dr. Wendy Chung's group on a broad range of projects, including congenital heart disease (CHD), congenital diaphragmatic hernia (CDH, DHREAMS study), and neurodevelopmental disorders.
