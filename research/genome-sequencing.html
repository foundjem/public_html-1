Genome sequencing

Develop computational and statistical methods for analyzing genome sequencing data and optimizing study design in genetic studies. Ongoing projects include detecting copy number variants from exome or genome sequencing data, optimizing detection of short indels from exome and genome sequencing data, and detection of subclonal somatic mutations by 3rd-generation sequencing methods.
